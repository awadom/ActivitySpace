Background

A hackathon is an event, usually hosted by a tech company or organization, where programmers get together for a short period of time to collaborate on a project. Participants work quickly to achieve their task, as the events generally only last 24 to 48 hours. People with technical and non-technical backgrounds come together, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively create a unique solution from scratch — these generally take shape in the form of websites, mobile apps, and robots.

What To Do

Start by signing up at DevPost or Major League Hacking so that you can find a hackathon you are interested in. Currently there is a three week hackathon to combat COVID-19, check out HackQuarantine for more details.
