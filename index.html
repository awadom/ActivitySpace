Why Social Distancing?

Even if it's no big deal for you, it can be deadly for others. There are not only people who are immunosupressed or elderly, but those who have asthma or are pregnant. By staying home, you're reducing the spread of coronavirus and freeing up hospital beds for those who truly need it.

But It's So Lonely

Cabin fever may not be a psychological disorder, but the feelings with it are very real. To be in the same house for days or weeks on end can leave a person itching for something new. Even though you may not be able to leave for leisure, you can still find new things to do every day!

What Can I Do?

Here at Activities Space, we have a plethora of activities for you to choose regardless of how you're social distancing. Have kids at home? Want some alone time? Interested in reaching out to friends? We have activities for it all. Just scroll down!

About
#us

In the last few weeks, COVID-19 took the world by surprise. Schools were abruptly shut down, jobs were quickly lost, and toilet paper was feverously hoarded. Now, with stay-home policies put in place, we are only permitted to leave for groceries and work. We have become a country that stays inside alone. "Flatten the curve" is something we hear every day, but it's hard to isolate yourself. Activities Space .highlights. the opportunities we still have to connect. With a computer in every household, social distancing doesn't have to be so lonely.
